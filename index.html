About Me

This is my about me section: Tyna William is a software engineer bla bla who obviously enjoys speaking about herself in the third person. Fun Fact: I started my college career as a dual major in biochemistry and molecular biology. Whilst I've always loved the sciences, what I didn't love is the lack of room for creative expression during an undergrad career. I wanted to do something where I could see the products of my hard work manifest in front of me ... enter computer science. From my first "hello world" program, I fell in love with the creativity computer science afforded me. I became a CS major and never looked back. After college, I decided I wanted to feel more "work ready" before I could call myself a software engineer, and so I went on to pursue a software engineering track with App Academy. Now here we are!
